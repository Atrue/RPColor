Welcome!

Nick must be not a null!

  • Keys 1,2 and 3 shoot red, green and blue paints. You can feed other players or deal damage to the boss.
  • Stage 1 (Q)
    Impulse.
    Shoot 3 colorful paint. Gives small impulse

    Shield.
    Gives you invulnerability for 4 seconds.

    Speedy.
    Increases your speed to 50% for 5 seconds.
  • Stage 2 (W)
    Fire bomb.
    Shoot a bomb. It deals 10 % Red paint damage.

    Hook.
    Shoot a hook-bomb. It clings first enemy and pulls it to you and deals 4% Green paint damage.

    Toss.
    Shoot a toss-bomb. It clings first enemy and pulls you to it. Deals 7% Blue paint damage.
  • Stage 3 (E)
    Edible paint.
    Adds 20% from your max count of paint to your mass.

    Stun bomb.
    Create a bomb behind you for 15 seconds. It stuns max for 3 seconds.

    Slow bomb.
    Throw a bomb that slows enemy for 50%. It deals 2% Blue paint damage.
  • Stage 4 (R)
    Blink.
    Smoothly blink to short distance.

    Reincarnation (passive).
    Revives you after death in random point.

    Invisibility.
    Gives you invisible for 15 seconds.
